About Me

Hi, I’m Edz Lilian Galdones, a Certified Xero ProAdvisor, Certified QuickBooks ProAdvisor, and Intuit-trained bookkeeper with over 4 years of experience. I hold a bachelor’s degree in accounting and have worked extensively with both U.S. and Canadian clients.

My expertise covers full-charge bookkeeping, IFRS and GAAP compliance, tax filings (T1, T2, T4), and handling HST/GST. I’ve successfully managed books for 20+ clients, ensuring accuracy, clarity, and timely reporting.

My goal is to make bookkeeping stress-free for business owners, so they can focus on growth while having reliable financial insights.
